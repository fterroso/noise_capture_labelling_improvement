Use your location

To place the noise measurement on a map, the NoiseCapture application needs to collect the position (GPS coordinates) of your smartphone.

This location service is active from the moment you start a measurement, whether the application is in the foreground or in the background. As soon as you stop the measurement, the location service ends immediately.\n\n This data is completely anonymous and is not used for advertising or commercial purposes. Moreover, if the user agrees, these measurements are shared in a community database, freely accessible and reusable.
